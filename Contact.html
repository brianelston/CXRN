Information for contrast reaction management training simulation.





Instructions:

1) Play the movie in the scenario. The patient's background will be displayed to the right of the movie.
2) Below the movie are options for Vital signs, Pulse oximetry, and Clinical exam/Stethoscope findings.
3) The button to the right 'Help/End' can be clicked if you want to end the scenario which will bring up limited learning points of the case. Clicking on the learning points button brings up the complete learning points. Please only click these buttons if you are finished with the scenario and do not wish to proceed any further.
4) The vitals, technologist actions, medication selections along with patient and technologist responses will be displayed below the above described buttons.
5) A transcription log of the scenario's progress is displayed in the box at the bottom of the page.
6) The 'Restart Procedure' button in the upper right hand corner allows you to restart the scenario without having to watch the video again.
